Gasworks Park

Gasworks Park
2101 N Northlake Way
Seattle, WA 98101

Description: A 19.1-acre park on the site of the former Seattle Gas Light Company gasification plant, Gas Works park contains remnants of the sole remaining coal gasification plant in the United States. Spend the day flying kites, picnicing, exploring the displays, or simply enjoying the park!



Discovery Park

Discovery Park
2101 N Northlake Way
Seattle, WA 98101

Description: Discovery Park is a 534-acre park on the shores of Puget Sound in the Magnolia neighborhood of Seattle. It is the city's largest public park and contains 11.81 miles of walking trails. Come spend a day exploring the trails of the park and taking in the views.



Rattlesnake Ridge

Rattlesnake Trailhead
North Bend, WA 98045

Description: One of Seattle's favorite hikes, Rattlesnake ridge offers a stunning view of Rattlesnake Lake and the Cascades in just a 4 mile round trip hike!



Snow Lake

Rattlesnake Trailhead
Alpental Access rd, North Bend, WA 98045

Description: The most visited lake in the stunning Alpine Lakes Wilderness, Snow Lake offers a easy access trailhead and rewarding hike, making it one of the most popular in the Seattle area.
