Seattle Pride Parade 2019

March

Westlake Park
401 Pine St
Seattle, WA 98104

June 30, 2019
11:00am - 4:00pm

Description: The parade includes more than 200 participating groups and 60,000 individuals marching in support of LGBTQ pride. This year the parade will commemorate the 50th anniversary of the Stonewall Rebellion, which became a national movement and eventually led to marches around the country, including the first Seattle Pride march in 1974.

This is a FREE event to attend!
