Seattle Pride Parade 2019

March

Westlake Park
401 Pine St
Seattle, WA 98104

June 30, 2019
11:00am - 4:00pm

Description: The parade includes more than 200 participating groups and 60,000 individuals marching in support of LGBTQ pride. This year the parade will commemorate the 50th anniversary of the Stonewall Rebellion, which became a national movement and eventually led to marches around the country, including the first Seattle Pride march in 1974.

This is a FREE event to attend!



Nordic Sól - A Summer Celebration of Contemporary Arctic Culture

Celebration

Nordic Museum
2655 NW Market St
Seattle, WA 98107

August 22-25, 2019

Description: The National Nordic Museum’s new summer cultural showcase, Nordic Sól, is here! With a cool focus on Arctic vendors, crafts, music, and culture, this collaboration of polar artisans and like-minded local businesses will feature a series of thoughtfully curated events, including an Arctic-themed PechaKucha, Friday night concert featuring the Circumpolar Hip Hop Collab, and a Saturday evening Arctic Adventure of modern fashion, traditional foods, and aquavit garden! Kids crafts and family-friendly activities throughout the weekend!

This is a FREE event to attend, but some exhibits require paid tickets.

Dia de los Muertos 2019

Celebration

Seattle Center
305 Harrison St
Seattle, WA 98109

November 2, 2019
6:30am - 11:30pm

Description: This is a time to celebrate, not a day to mourn. People celebrate by placing an offering with food, fruit, and other offering the departed liked when he or she was alive. Also, there is music and colorful ornaments placed around the offering to welcome the spirit of the diseased.

This is a FREE event to attend!
